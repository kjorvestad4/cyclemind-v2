Chapter 7, “Perimenopause.” 
Conclusion 
Clinicians treating women for mental health conditions at times of reproductive tran-
sition must have a firm understanding of the hormonal and other physiological 
changes that accompany such transitions. This chapter offered an overview of the phys-
ical changes that accompany the menstrual cycle, pregnancy, parturition and the post-
partum period, lactation, and menopause. The mental health sequelae of these changes 
are the subject of the remaining chapters of this book.
