Sociodemographic Considerations 
Populations who may be predisposed to develop perinatal PTSD include women of 
younger age; women who lack support from the father of the baby, partner, or family; 
single parents; women with a preexistin g psychiatric disord er; Black women and 
women of other racial minorities; and LGBTQ+ individuals (Capaldi et al. 2012; Vig-
nato et al. 2018). Psychosocial stressors, such as homelessness, food and financial inse-
curity, and exposure to violence, can aggr avate symptoms of PTSD (Capaldi et al. 
2012). Conversely, factors favoring a good prognosis include a shorter duration of 
trauma symptoms, good premorbid functioning, and strong social supports (Capaldi
