Key Points 
• No single diagnosis or treatment can be used for all patients with any one of 
these gynecological conditions. 
• The multiple underlying causes, diagnostic techniques, and treatments re -
quire providers to take additional time with each patient to get to the root of 
the problem(s) and find helpful treatments. 
• When supporting the mental health of a woman experiencing gynecological 
conditions, clinicians should work together with the patient and a multidisci -
plinary team to reach the best outcome. 
• Mental health professionals should be sensitive to the complex nature of the 
physical experiences of pain, infertility, abnormal bleeding, and sexual dys -
function, as well as the psychological toll that these gynecological conditions 
take on women and their families.
