581 Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder 
• Cognitive-behavioral therapy with exposure and response prevention re -
mains the gold standard for treating OCD in the perinatal period. 
• Selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors and tricyclic antidepressants can be 
effective but generally require higher doses for OCD than for depression.
