Chapter 23, “Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder.” Lis-
dexamfetamine is the first medication to receive an FDA indication for BED, with sup-
porting studies demonstrating significant reductions in binge eating frequency and 
improvement in Clinical Global Impressions scores (McElroy et al. 2015). Although it 
does not have specific indications, lisdexamfetamine is also associated with decreas-
ing weight, BMI, and triglyceride levels (Davis and Attia 2019). The lipase inhibitor 
orlistat has been studied in BED, showing benefit in weight loss and reducing Eating 
Disorder Inventory–2 scores, although not for reducing binge-eating frequency. Chro-
mium has also been studied in BED, with moderate doses demonstrating improved 
glycemic control (not seen with placebo or high-dose groups), although further study
