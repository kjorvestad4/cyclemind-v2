With dissociative symptoms: The individual’s symptoms meet the criteria for posttrau-

of either of the following: 
1. Depersonalization: Persistent or recurrent experiences of feeling detached from,

Intimate Partner Violence/Sexual Trauma 
The symptoms of IPV and sexual trauma ma nifest within several distinct domains 
(Chisholm et al. 2017): 
• Physical: Victim experiences hitting, slapping, punching, shoving, biting, choking, 
or the use of weapons. 
• Psychological: Victim experiences threats of vi olence, intimidation, humiliation, 
feeling controlled, being socially isolated, being stalked, being forced into illegal ac-
tivity or into substance use. 
• Sexual: Victim experiences being forced or coerced into sexual acts in which the 
person does not want to participate, sabotage of contraception, being coerced into 
pregnancy or abortion, and intentionally being infected with sexually transmitted 
infections.
