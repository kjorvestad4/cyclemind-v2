Treatment of Female Sexual Dysfunctions 
Treatment of FSDs is often best approached in a multidisciplinary manner. The ISSWSH 
process-of-care model also outlines management and treatment strategies (Clayton et 
al. 2018; Parish et al. 2019). Across FSD types, core-level treatment approaches include 
education on normal sexual functioning and modification of biopsychosocial factors af-
fecting sexual function (e.g., cognitive therapy to alter negative beliefs or guilt about 
sexuality; reducing performance anxiety; addressing relationship conflict; education re-
garding use of vaginal lubricants to address dryness; addressing GSM). Advanced-level 
treatments may require referral to one or more specialists.
