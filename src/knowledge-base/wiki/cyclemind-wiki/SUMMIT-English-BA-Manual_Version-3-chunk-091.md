In delivering SUMMIT BA, several types of safety concerns may arise. It is important to have processes 
and procedures for managing these concerns in place prior to delivering SUMMIT BA.

Depending on your setting, these processes and procedures may rely on resources and protocols 
internal to your setting (for example, if you work in a hospital), external to your setting (for example, if 
you are based in a community center), or a combination of internal and external resources. The 
information in this section provides general guidance about recognizing and responding to safety 
concerns.
